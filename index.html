Slide-On Cable Markers

Cable markers are commonly used to label and identify cables and wires, reducing the time it takes to troubleshoot, repair and maintain, whilst also enhancing the safety of electrical installations. We stock a variety of sizes, colours, materials and affixing methods including slide-on, clip-on, tie-on, self-adhesive, and heat shrink, for pre or post termination applications.
